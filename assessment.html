How Do You Read the Room?

A 35-sentence emotional baseline assessment

You will see 35 situations -- things that happen to people. Some are clearly bad. Some are clearly good. Some could go either way.


Rate each one based on how it would make you feel if it happened to you. Not what you think it means objectively -- how it lands in your gut.


There are no wrong answers. This measures your emotional interpretation patterns -- how you read situations when the facts are the same for everyone.
